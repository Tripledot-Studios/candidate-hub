About Tripledot Studios

Tripledot Studios was founded in 2017 by industry veterans from King, Peak Games, Facebook, and Product Madness — united by one goal: build a mobile games company driven by data and genuine product craft. We make casual, free-to-play games that are accessible to players of all levels, and we work on them obsessively.

We're a highly profitable, zero-debt business with 430+ people across six global offices. In 2025, we closed a transformative $800M acquisition of AppLovin's mobile gaming portfolio — making us one of the most significant forces in mobile games globally.

What to expect

Our interview process

We keep our process clear and purposeful — no surprises, no tricks. Here's the full picture and where you are right now and what the next steps will look like.
Our commitment to you
We give feedback at every stage and aim to move quickly. You'll hear from us within a few working days of each step — and if anything changes, we'll let you know proactively. No ghosting, ever.
1
Talent Acquisition Interview
A 30-minute conversation with our TA team to get to know each other, explore your background, and share more about the role. This is a two-way conversation — bring your questions.
You are here
2
Hiring Manager Interview
A deeper conversation with the hiring manager covering your experience, technical skills, and how you work. Expect to discuss specific examples from your career and walk through your approach to real problems.
3
Take-Home Assessment
A short task or case study relevant to the role, completed in your own time. We keep it focused and respectful of your time — typically no more than 2–3 hours.
4
Panel Interview
A collaborative discussion with several members of the team, giving you the opportunity to meet potential colleagues and stakeholders. This round focuses on how you work with others, your communication style, and how your experience and technical skills align with the team’s needs.
Get ready

How to prepare

Our interviews are a conversation — not a test. Come as you are, with a clear sense of what you're looking for and why this role caught your eye.
Know your story
Walk through your career clearly and concisely — what you've done, what you've learned, and why you're exploring a move. Focus on what's most relevant to this role.
Know what you want
We'll ask what you're looking for in your next role. Be honest — there are no right answers. The clearer you are about your goals, the better we can assess whether this is a genuine match.
Be specific about your impact
When sharing examples — what was the situation, what did you specifically do, and what was the outcome? The clearer your examples, the easier it is for us to advocate for you internally.
Try our games
Download Woodoku, Solitaire or Triple Tile from the App Store or Google Play. You don’t need to be an expert — but genuine curiosity about the product always comes through.
Bring your questions
Good questions signal how you think. Consider: what does success look like in year one? How does the team make decisions? What's the biggest challenge this hire will face in the first 90 days?
Common questions

FAQs

When will I hear back after an interview? +
We aim to get back to you within a few working days of each stage. If anything changes we'll always let you know proactively.
Can I ask about salary during the talent screen? +
Absolutely — we're happy to discuss compensation expectations early so there are no surprises later.
What's the culture actually like? +
Collaborative, fast-moving, and data-driven. We work hard and take the work seriously — but we're friendly, low on hierarchy, and genuinely care about doing good work together.
Is Tripledot financially stable? +
Yes — we're a highly profitable, zero-debt business. We've grown from 0 to 430+ people in 8 years without external funding pressure.
How does the company approach AI? +
We actively embrace AI as a tool to enhance productivity, creativity, and efficiency across teams. We encourage thoughtful experimentation and adoption of AI where it adds value, while maintaining high standards for quality, ethics, and human oversight.
Do you offer remote or hybrid work? +
We operate a hybrid model, with a mix of in-office collaboration and remote flexibility, depending on the role and location.
Do you offer visa sponsorship? +
We offer visa sponsorship for some roles in select EU locations. At this time, we do not provide visa sponsorship for North America.
Do you offer relocation support? +
Relocation support may be available depending on the role and location - this can be discussed during the hiring process.
What benefits do you offer? +
We offer a competitive benefits package that includes health coverage, paid time off, wellness support, and additional perks depending on location. Refer to the job description for the list of benefits applicable to the role or reach out to your talent partner for more specific information.
I have accessibility needs — who should I contact? +
Please reach out to your Talent Partner who will make sure we can accommodate your needs throughout the process.
