About Tripledot Studios

Tripledot Studios was founded in 2017 by industry veterans from King, Peak Games, Facebook, and Product Madness — united by one goal: build a mobile games company driven by data and genuine product craft. We make casual, free-to-play games that are accessible to players of all levels, and we work on them obsessively.

We're a highly profitable, zero-debt business with 430+ people across six global offices. In 2025, we closed a transformative $800M acquisition of AppLovin's mobile gaming portfolio — making us one of the most significant forces in mobile games globally.

Our interview process

Getting ready

How to prepare

The talent screen is a conversation — not a test. Come as you are, with a clear sense of what you're looking for and why this role caught your eye.

🔖 Know your story
Walk through your career clearly and concisely — what you've done, what you've learned, and why you're exploring a move. Focus on what's most relevant to this role.
🎮 Try our games
Download Woodoku, Solitaire, or Triple Tile from the App Store or Google Play. You don't need to be an expert — but genuine curiosity about the product always comes through.
🧭 Know what you want
We'll ask what you're looking for in your next role. Be honest — there are no right answers. The clearer you are about your goals, the better we can assess whether this is a genuine match.
💬 Bring your questions
Good questions signal how you think. Consider: what does success look like in year one? How does the team make decisions? What's the biggest challenge this hire will face in the first 90 days?
📌 Be specific about your impact
When sharing examples — what was the situation, what did you specifically do, and what was the outcome? The clearer your examples, the easier it is for us to advocate for you internally.

30 minutes. We'll keep to time and leave space for your questions at the end.
Do I need to prepare anything in advance? +
No — this is a conversational stage. No presentation or task required. Just come ready to talk through your background and what you're looking for.

What's the culture actually like? +
We're a team of experienced people who take the work seriously but not themselves. Direct communication, genuine collaboration, and curiosity. With 35+ nationalities across six offices, strong ideas win regardless of where they come from.

Yes — we're a highly profitable business with zero debt and a strong balance sheet. We've grown consistently since 2017, and the 2025 AppLovin acquisition significantly expands the scale and ambition of the group.
